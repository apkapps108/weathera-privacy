Privacy Policy for Weathra

Last Updated: December 26, 2025

Weathra ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use the Weathra mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

A. Location Information

To provide accurate local weather forecasts and city-level information, Weathra may request access to your device’s location (fine or coarse location).

  • Automatic Collection: Latitude and longitude when permission is granted
  • Purpose: Used only to display weather information relevant to your area

B. Search Queries

When you search for a city or location in the app, your search query is processed to return relevant weather results.

C. No Personal Identification

Weathra does not collect or store personally identifiable information, including name, email address, phone number, contacts, or account credentials. The app does not require user registration.

2. How We Use Information

  • Display weather forecasts and conditions
  • Provide location-based results
  • Improve performance using temporary local caching
  • Reduce unnecessary network requests

3. Data Storage and Retention

  • Local Caching: Data may be stored temporarily on your device (typically up to 10 minutes)
  • No Server-Side Storage: We do not store location or search history on our servers
  • No Tracking: Users are not tracked across apps or websites

4. Third-Party Services

Weathra uses external APIs to provide weather forecasts and location-based search functionality. These services receive only the minimum data required to process your request.

Weathra does not control how third-party services handle data. Users are encouraged to review their respective privacy policies.

5. Permissions

Weathra may request location access to show weather for your current location. You can enable or disable permissions at any time via your device settings. Disabling permissions may limit certain features.

6. Children’s Privacy

Weathra does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
